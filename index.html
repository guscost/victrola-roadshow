The Victrola Roadshow



This website is a place for people interested in knowing about the invention of the phonograph, particularly the Victrola.

Here you can learn some things that will be of help in providing information about this great forerunner of home music entertainment.



I have a very beautiful Victrola model VV 80 which can play the records that I found in various flea markets, thrift stores, antique shops and garage sales.

I have made a comprehensive index of all of the records. It can be used to find any record and performer.



Please contact me at the e-mail address listed below if you would like to hear a song. Once I have made a YouTube of the song, I can publish a list of the songs and make them available to others. It's that simple.

A copy of the index is free and available upon request.

- Emil J. Cost, founder of The Victrola Roadshow
